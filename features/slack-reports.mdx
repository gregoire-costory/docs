Extend cost visibility to where engineering work happens.

## How to set up

Steps:

1. Open or create a Saved View
2. Click **Schedule Report**
3. Pick the channel, cadence, and comparison period

## Use cases

Perfect for:

- Squad cost updates
- SRE / platform reviews
- Monthly leadership briefings
